      Why build a site for this?

      Because mythology needs receipts

      Friend leagues are funniest when everyone agrees to take them way too seriously. The second there is a roster page, a stat leaderboard, and a tape archive, the whole thing stops feeling like random runs and starts feeling like a living season.

      This site turns game nights into history. It gives your league a place for legends, slander, evidence, redemption arcs, and entirely too much analysis.
